## Page 162: Figure It Out

**Q1. How many whole units in $\frac{7}{2}$?**
**Answer:** 3 whole units ($\frac{6}{2} = 3$), with $\frac{1}{2}$ remaining.

**Q2. Whole units in $\frac{4}{3}$ and $\frac{7}{3}$?**
*   $\frac{4}{3}$: 1 whole unit.
*   $\frac{7}{3}$: 2 whole units.
