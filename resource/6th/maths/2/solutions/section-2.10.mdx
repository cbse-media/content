**Q7. Puzzle:**
I am an acute angle.
*   $\times 2 =$ Acute
*   $\times 3 =$ Acute
*   $\times 4 =$ Acute
*   $\times 5 =$ Obtuse

**Solution:**
The angle must be between $18^\circ$ and $22.5^\circ$.
Possible integer answers: **19째, 20째, 21째, 22째**.
*   Check 22: $22 \times 4 = 88$ (Acute). $22 \times 5 = 110$ (Obtuse). Correct.
*   Check 18: $18 \times 4 = 72$ (Acute). $18 \times 5 = 90$ (Right, not obtuse). Incorrect.
