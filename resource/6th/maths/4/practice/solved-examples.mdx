## Example 1: Smriti's Runs (Bar Graph Scale)

**Problem:**
Smriti scored the following runs in 8 matches:
80, 50, 10, 100, 90, 0, 90, 50.

**Task:** Draw a bar graph.

**Solution Analysis:**
1.  **Range:** The scores range from 0 to 100.
2.  **Scale Selection:**
    *   If we take 1 unit = 1 run, the graph will be 100 units tall (too big).
    *   If we take 1 unit = 10 runs, the graph will be 10 units tall (perfect).
3.  **Drawing:**
    *   **Match 1 (80):** Draw bar up to 8 units.
    *   **Match 2 (50):** Draw bar up to 5 units.
    *   **Match 3 (10):** Draw bar up to 1 unit.
    *   **Match 4 (100):** Draw bar up to 10 units.
    *   **Match 6 (0):** No bar (height is 0).

## Example 2: Interpreting Tally Marks

**Problem:**
A teacher marked attendance using tally marks. For a specific day, the mark was: `||||` `||||` `||`.
How many students were present?

**Solution:**
1.  Identify complete bundles: There are 2 bundles of `||||` (crossed).
    *   $2 \times 5 = 10$.
2.  Identify loose lines: There are 2 loose lines `||`.
    *   $10 + 2 = 12$.
3.  **Answer:** 12 students.
