## 1. Fill in the boxes with < or >

*   **a. -2 [ ] +5** $\rightarrow$ $-2 < +5$ (Negative is less than positive)
*   **b. -5 [ ] +4** $\rightarrow$ $-5 < +4$
*   **c. -5 [ ] -3** $\rightarrow$ $-5 < -3$ (-5 is lower down/more left than -3)
*   **d. +6 [ ] -6** $\rightarrow$ $+6 > -6$
*   **e. 0 [ ] -4** $\rightarrow$ $0 > -4$ (0 is above any negative)
*   **f. 0 [ ] +4** $\rightarrow$ $0 < +4$
