## Figure it Out - Large Numbers

*   **a. (+40) + \_\_\_\_ = +200**
    *   Start at 40, need to reach 200. Move Up 160.
    *   Answer: **+160**

*   **b. (+40) + \_\_\_\_ = -200**
    *   Start at 40, need to reach -200. Move Down 240.
    *   Answer: **-240**

*   **c. (-50) + \_\_\_\_ = +200**
    *   Start at -50, reach 200. Move Up 250.
    *   Answer: **+250**

*   **d. (-50) + \_\_\_\_ = -200**
    *   Start at -50, reach -200. Move Down 150.
    *   Answer: **-150**

*   **e. (-200) - (-40) =**
    *   Think: From -40 to -200. Move Down 160.
    *   Answer: **-160**

*   **f. (+200) - (+40) =**
    *   Think: From 40 to 200. Move Up 160.
    *   Answer: **+160**

*   **g. (-200) - (+40) =**
    *   Think: From 40 to -200. Move Down 240.
    *   Answer: **-240**
