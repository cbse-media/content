## Page 259: Banking

**1. Credits and Debits**
*   **Start:** 0
*   **Credits (+):** 30, 40, 50 $\rightarrow$ Total +120
*   **Debits (-):** 40, 50, 60 $\rightarrow$ Total -150
*   **Balance:** $120 - 150 = -30$.
*   **Answer:** ₹-30.

**2. Powers of 2 Debits**
*   **Debits:** 1, 2, 4, 8, 16, 32, 64, 128.
    *   Sum of these debits: $1+2+4+8+16+32+64+128 = 255$.
    *   Total Debit: -255.
*   **Credit:** +256.
*   **Balance:** $-255 + 256 = +1$.
*   **Answer:** ₹1.
