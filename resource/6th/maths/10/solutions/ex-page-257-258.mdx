## Page 257: Addition

**1. Complete the additions:**
*   **a. (+6) + (+4) = +10**
*   **b. (-3) + (-2) = -5** (3 negatives and 2 negatives make 5 negatives)
*   **c. (+5) + (-7) = -2** (5 positives cancel 5 negatives, leaving 2 negatives)
*   **d. (-2) + (+6) = +4** (2 negatives cancel 2 positives, leaving 4 positives)

**2. Cancel Zero Pairs:**
*   **a.** 3 Positives, 5 Negatives.
    *   3 pairs cancel. Left with 2 Negatives.
    *   **Result: -2**
*   **b.** 6 Positives, 3 Negatives.
    *   3 pairs cancel. Left with 3 Positives.
    *   **Result: +3**
