## Question
**What do you press to go four floors up? What do you press to go three floors down?**

### Solution
1.  **To go four floors up:**
    *   Each press of `+` moves you up one floor.
    *   We need to press `+` four times.
    *   Expression: $+4$ or $++++$.

2.  **To go three floors down:**
    *   Each press of `-` moves you down one floor.
    *   We need to press `-` three times.
    *   Expression: $-3$ or $---$.
