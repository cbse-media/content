## Question 1
**Represent in Egyptian System:**

*   **10458:**
    *   1 Finger (10,000)
    *   4 Coils (400)
    *   5 Heels (50)
    *   8 Strokes (8)
*   **1023:** 1 Lotus, 2 Heels, 3 Strokes.
*   **2660:** 2 Lotuses, 6 Coils, 6 Heels.
*   **784:** 7 Coils, 8 Heels, 4 Strokes.
