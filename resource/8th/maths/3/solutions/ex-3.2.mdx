## Question 1
**Represent the following in Roman system:**

**(i) 1222**
*   1000 = M
*   200 = CC
*   20 = XX
*   2 = II
*   **Answer:** MCCXXII

**(ii) 2999**
*   2000 = MM
*   900 = CM (1000 - 100)
*   90 = XC (100 - 10)
*   9 = IX (10 - 1)
*   **Answer:** MMCMXCIX

**(iii) 302**
*   300 = CCC
*   2 = II
*   **Answer:** CCCII

**(iv) 715**
*   700 = DCC (500 + 100 + 100)
*   10 = X
*   5 = V
*   **Answer:** DCCXV
