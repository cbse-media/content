## Multiplying by 11

**Evaluate:**
**(i) $94 \times 11$**
$$ 4 \rightarrow (9+4=13) \rightarrow (9+1=10) $$
$$ 1034 $$

**(ii) $495 \times 11$**
$$ 5 \rightarrow (9+5=14) \rightarrow (4+9+1=14) \rightarrow (4+1=5) $$
$$ 5445 $$

**(iii) $3279 \times 11$**
$$ 9 \rightarrow (7+9=16) \rightarrow (2+7+1=10) \rightarrow (3+2+1=6) \rightarrow 3 $$
$$ 36069 $$

## Multiplying by 101

**General Rule:**
Multiply by 100 then add the number itself.
$$ N \times 101 = N(100+1) = 100N + N $$
For a 2-digit number $ab$, $ab \times 101 = abab$.

**(i) $89 \times 101 = 8989$**

**(ii) $949 \times 101$**
$$ = 94900 + 949 = 95849 $$

## Multiplying by 1001

**(iii) $265831 \times 1001$**
$$ = 265831000 + 265831 = 266096831 $$

**(iv) $1111 \times 1001$**
$$ = 1111000 + 1111 = 1112111 $$

## Multiplying by 99

**(v) $9734 \times 99$**
$$ = 9734(100 - 1) = 973400 - 9734 $$
$$ = 963666 $$
