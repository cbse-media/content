## 4. Calendar 2x2 Square
Let numbers be:
$$
\begin{matrix}
x & x+1 \\
x+7 & x+8
\end{matrix}
$$
Diagonals:
1.  $x(x+8) = x^2 + 8x$
2.  $(x+1)(x+7) = x^2 + 7x + x + 7 = x^2 + 8x + 7$

**Observation:** The product of the secondary diagonal (top-right to bottom-left) is always **7 more** than the main diagonal.
$55 - 48 = 7$. Confirmed.

## 6. Remainders with 7
Let $N_1 = 7a + 3$ and $N_2 = 7b + 5$.
*   **Sum:** $(7a+3) + (7b+5) = 7(a+b) + 8 = 7(a+b+1) + 1$.
    *   Remainder: **1**.
*   **Difference:** $(7a+3) - (7b+5)$.
    *   Wait, use modular arithmetic logic. $3 - 5 = -2 \equiv 5 \pmod 7$.
    *   Remainder: **5**.
*   **Product:** $(3)(5) = 15 = 14 + 1$.
    *   Remainder: **1**.

## 10. Dhauli Park Area
Total Area = $(g+g+w) \times (g+w) = (2g+w)(g+w)$.
Green Area = $2g^2$.
Tiled Area = Total - Green.
$$ (2g^2 + 2gw + gw + w^2) - 2g^2 $$
$$ = 3gw + w^2 $$
$$ = w(3g + w) $$
