## Figure it Out: Sums and Differences (Page 30 / PDF 75)

**1. Find the sums:**

**(a) $5.3 + 2.6$**
$$ = 7.9 $$

**(b) $18 + 8.8$**
Align properly: $18.0 + 8.8 = \mathbf{26.8}$

**(c) $2.15 + 5.26$**
$$ = 7.41 $$

**(d) $9.01 + 9.10$**
$$ = 18.11 $$

**(e) $29.19 + 9.91$**
$19 + 91$ hundredths $= 110$ hundredths $= 1.10$.
$29 + 9 + 1.10 = \mathbf{39.10}$ (or $39.1$)

**(f) $0.934 + 0.6$**
Align properly: $0.934 + 0.600 = \mathbf{1.534}$

**(g) $0.75 + 0.03$**
$$ = 0.78 $$

**(h) $6.236 + 0.487$**
$$ = 6.723 $$

---

**2. Find the differences:**

**(a) $5.6 - 2.3$**
$$ = 3.3 $$

**(b) $18 - 8.8$**
Align: $18.0 - 8.8$. Borrow $1$ from $18 \to 17.10$. $17.10 - 8.8 = \mathbf{9.2}$

**(c) $10.4 - 4.5$**
$10.4 - 4.5 = \mathbf{5.9}$

**(d) $17 - 16.198$**
Align: $17.000 - 16.198 = \mathbf{0.802}$

**(e) $17 - 0.05$**
Align: $17.00 - 0.05 = \mathbf{16.95}$

**(f) $34.505 - 18.1$**
Align: $34.505 - 18.100 = \mathbf{16.405}$

**(g) $9.9 - 9.09$**
Align: $9.90 - 9.09 = \mathbf{0.81}$

**(h) $6.236 - 0.487$**
$$ = 5.749 $$

## Decimal Sequences (Page 31 / PDF 76)

**Identify the change and write the next 3 terms for each sequence.**

**(a) $4.4, 4.45, 4.5, \dots$**
*   **Change:** Increasing by $0.05$ ($4.45 - 4.40 = 0.05$)
*   **Next 3 terms:** $4.55, 4.6, 4.65$

**(b) $25.75, 26.25, 26.75, \dots$**
*   **Change:** Increasing by $0.50$
*   **Next 3 terms:** $27.25, 27.75, 28.25$

**(c) $10.56, 10.67, 10.78, \dots$**
*   **Change:** Increasing by $0.11$
*   **Next 3 terms:** $10.89, 11.00, 11.11$

**(d) $13.5, 16, 18.5, \dots$**
*   **Change:** Increasing by $2.5$
*   **Next 3 terms:** $21.0, 23.5, 26.0$

**(e) $8.5, 9.4, 10.3, \dots$**
*   **Change:** Increasing by $0.9$
*   **Next 3 terms:** $11.2, 12.1, 13.0$

**(f) $5, 4.95, 4.90, \dots$**
*   **Change:** Decreasing by $0.05$ (Subtracting $0.05$)
*   **Next 3 terms:** $4.85, 4.80, 4.75$

**(g) $12.45, 11.95, 11.45, \dots$**
*   **Change:** Decreasing by $0.50$
*   **Next 3 terms:** $10.95, 10.45, 9.95$

**(h) $36.5, 33, 29.5, \dots$**
