## Scenario 1: The Honeybee's Anatomy
The lengths of the body parts of a honeybee are given below. Let's find its total length by adding fractions and decimals!
*   **Head:** $2 \frac{3}{10} \text{ units}$
*   **Thorax:** $5 \frac{4}{10} \text{ units}$
*   **Abdomen:** $7 \frac{5}{10} \text{ units}$

**Method 1: Using Decimals**
Convert the fractions to decimals first.
*   Head: $2.3$
*   Thorax: $5.4$
*   Abdomen: $7.5$

Add them up:
$2.3 + 5.4 = 7.7$
$7.7 + 7.5 = \mathbf{15.2 \text{ units}}$

**Method 2: Using Fractions**
Add the whole numbers: $2 + 5 + 7 = 14$
Add the tenths: $\frac{3}{10} + \frac{4}{10} + \frac{5}{10} = \frac{12}{10}$
Combine: $14 + \frac{12}{10} = 14 + 1 \frac{2}{10} = \mathbf{15 \frac{2}{10} \text{ units}}$ (which is $15.2$!)

## Scenario 2: Measuring Fingers
The length of Shylaja’s hand is $12 \frac{4}{10} \text{ units}$, and her palm is $6 \frac{7}{10} \text{ units}$. What is the length of her longest (middle) finger?

**Solution Strategy:**
The hand consists of the palm + the finger. 
So, $\text{Finger} = \text{Total Hand} - \text{Palm}$
$$ \text{Finger} = 12.4 - 6.7 $$

Let's use the decimal subtraction method:
$$ \begin{array}{r@{\quad}l} \color{red}{^{11}} \cancel{12}. \color{red}{^{14}} \cancel{4} & \\ - 6.7 & \\ \hline \mathbf{5.7} & \text{units} \end{array} $$

Her middle finger is **$5.7 \text{ units}$** long (or $5 \frac{7}{10}$).

## Scenario 3: The Tiny Fish
A Celestial Pearl Danio’s length is $2 \frac{4}{10} \text{ cm}$, and the length of a Philippine Goby is $\frac{9}{10} \text{ cm}$. What is the difference in their lengths?

**Solution Strategy:**
Convert to decimals to make subtraction easy!
*   Danio = $2.4 \text{ cm}$
*   Goby = $0.9 \text{ cm}$

$$ 2.4 - 0.9 = \mathbf{1.5 \text{ cm}} $$
The difference is $1.5 \text{ cm}$. 

<Callout variant="tip">
**Visualization:** 
$1.5 \text{ cm}$ is exactly $15 \text{ mm}$. If you look at a standard ruler, that's just a little bit wider than the tip of your pinky finger! These fish are incredibly small!
</Callout>
