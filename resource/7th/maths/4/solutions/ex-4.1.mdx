## Figure it Out (Page 84)

**1. Write formulas for the perimeter of:**

**(a) Triangle with all sides equal.**
*   **Solution:** Let the side length be $a$. A triangle has 3 sides. 
    Perimeter $= a + a + a = \mathbf{3a}$.

**(b) A regular pentagon.**
*   **Solution:** Let the side length be $a$. A regular pentagon has 5 equal sides. 
    Perimeter $= a + a + a + a + a = \mathbf{5a}$.

**(c) A regular hexagon.**
*   **Solution:** Let the side length be $a$. A regular hexagon has 6 equal sides. 
    Perimeter $= \mathbf{6a}$.

**2. Munirathna has a $20 \text{ m}$ long pipe. He joins another pipe of some length to this one. Give the expression for the combined length of the pipe. Use the letter-number '$k$' to denote the length in meters of the other pipe.**
*   **Solution:** 
    Length of first pipe $= 20 \text{ m}$.
    Length of second pipe $= k \text{ m}$.
    Combined length $= \mathbf{20 + k}$

**4. Venkatalakshmi owns a flour mill. It takes 10 seconds for the roller mill to start running. Once it is running, each kg of grain takes 8 seconds to grind into powder. Which of the expressions below describes the time taken to complete grind ‘$y$’ kg of grain, assuming the machine is off initially?**
(a) $10 + 8 + y$
(b) $(10 + 8) \times y$
(c) $10 \times 8 \times y$
(d) $10 + 8 \times y$
(e) $10 \times y + 8$

*   **Solution:** 
    Fixed start time $= 10$ seconds.
    Time to grind $y$ kg $= 8 \times y = 8y$ seconds.
    Total time = Fixed time + Grinding time $= 10 + 8y$.
    Matching option: **(d) $10 + 8 \times y$**

**5. Write algebraic expressions using letters of your choice.**

**(a) 5 more than a number**
*   **Solution:** Let the number be $x$.
    Expression: **$x + 5$**

**(b) 4 less than a number**
*   **Solution:** Let the number be $y$.
    Expression: **$y - 4$**

**(c) 2 less than 13 times a number**
*   **Solution:** Let the number be $m$.
    13 times the number $= 13m$.
    2 less than that $= \mathbf{13m - 2}$

**(d) 13 less than 2 times a number**
*   **Solution:** Let the number be $n$.
    2 times the number $= 2n$.
    13 less than that $= \mathbf{2n - 13}$

**7. In a calendar month, if any $2 \times 3$ grid full of dates is chosen, write expressions for the dates in the blank cells if the bottom middle cell has date '$w$'.**

If the grid looks like this:
|  |  |  |
| :---: | :---: | :---: |
| $?$ | $?$ | $?$ |
| $w-1$ | $w$ | $?$ |

*   **Solution:**
    In a calendar, moving horizontally changes the day by $+1$ or $-1$.
    Moving vertically changes the week by $+7$ or $-7$.
    *   Bottom right: One day after $w$ is **$w+1$**.
    *   Top middle: One week before $w$ is **$w-7$**.
    *   Top left: One day before top middle is $(w-7) - 1 =$ **$w-8$**.
    *   Top right: One day after top middle is $(w-7) + 1 =$ **$w-6$**.
