## Growing With Age

Around the age of 10–12, you or your friends might start noticing significant physical changes. This marks the onset of adolescence. Let's look at the most common observable changes.

### 1. Increase in Height and Body Structure
While we grow continuously from birth, the increase in height becomes much more prominent and rapid during adolescence. 
*   **Boys:** Along with growing taller, boys may notice their shoulders becoming broader, their chests getting wider, and a general gain in muscle strength.
*   **Girls:** Girls also undergo rapid changes in height and weight, along with specific body changes like the development of breasts.

### 2. Changes in Voice
Have you noticed that teenage boys often have voices that crack or sound hoarse? 
*   In adolescent boys, the growth of the voice box (larynx) leads to a deeper voice. This growth can often be seen as a bump in the throat region called the **Adam’s apple**. 
*   The voice box also grows in adolescent girls, but it is not as large, leading to only slight changes in their voice.

### 3. Appearance of Body Hair
Both boys and girls experience the growth of hair in different parts of their bodies, such as the armpits and the pubic region. 
*   Boys often develop facial hair, which later grows into a moustache and a beard. Some boys may also develop chest and back hair.

### 4. Changes in Facial Skin (Pimples and Acne)
A very common occurrence during the teenage years is the appearance of small, reddish pimples on the face.
*   **Why does this happen?** During adolescence, there is an increase in oily secretions from the skin (sebum). This excess oil can clog the skin pores and lead to infections, resulting in a condition called **acne**.

<Callout variant="warning">
**A Key Point to Remember:**
Whether it is a change in height, voice, or facial hair, it is crucial to recognize that the *timing*, *nature*, and *extent* of these changes vary greatly among individuals. These variations are completely normal. Do not compare your timeline to others!
