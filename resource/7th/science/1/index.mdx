## Introduction

Welcome to Grade 7 Science! After your adventures in Grade 6, we are ready to dive deeper into the wonderful world of science. This chapter is not just about memorizing facts; it is an invitation to question, perform experiments, and explore the universe.

The world of science covers everything—small and large, near and far. Whether we are looking at tiny cells inside a leaf, observing the movement of the sun and the stars, or testing the materials around us at home, curiosity leads the way.

<Callout variant="tip">
**Key Concept:** Science is a process—a way of thinking that welcomes curiosity, asks questions, and remains open to the unknown. 
</Callout>
