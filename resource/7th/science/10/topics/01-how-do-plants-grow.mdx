## The Essential Ingredients for Growth

When a seed germinates, it needs specific conditions to grow into a healthy plant. What are these conditions?

To find out, we can conduct a simple experiment with three identical plant saplings in pots:
*   **Pot A:** Placed in direct sunlight, watered regularly.
*   **Pot B:** Placed in direct sunlight, but completely deprived of water.
*   **Pot C:** Placed in a dark cupboard, watered regularly.

### The Results
After two weeks, what do you think will happen?
*   **Pot A** will show maximum growth. It will be taller, with more green leaves.
*   **Pot B** will likely wilt, turn brown, and die because it lacked water.
*   **Pot C** might survive briefly, but its growth will be stunted, its stem might become weak and pale, and its leaves will likely turn yellow and drop off because it lacked sunlight.

<Callout variant="tip">
**Conclusion:**
From this simple observation, we can confidently infer that **both sunlight and water are absolutely essential** for the healthy growth and survival of plants. 
</Callout>
